7. Messages and Communications

Ghost404 is designed with privacy as one of its core principles. Whenever technically possible, the application establishes direct peer-to-peer (P2P) communication between participating devices. This architecture minimizes reliance on centralized infrastructure and helps reduce the amount of information processed by our servers.

Ghost404 is designed not to permanently store the contents of private messages on our servers. The application attempts to deliver messages directly between users whenever possible.

8. Peer-to-Peer Communication

Ghost404 primarily uses peer-to-peer communication technologies, including WebRTC where supported, to establish secure connections between users.

Because peer-to-peer communication depends on factors outside our control, including Internet connectivity, NAT traversal, firewalls, mobile networks, VPNs, and carrier restrictions, a direct connection cannot be guaranteed in every situation.

When a peer-to-peer connection is successfully established, communication occurs directly between participating devices without permanently storing message content on Ghost404 servers.

9. Temporary Message Relay

If a direct peer-to-peer connection cannot be established, or if a user chooses an alternative connection method supported by the application, Ghost404 may temporarily relay encrypted messages using Firebase Realtime Database solely for message delivery.

These temporary relay messages are encrypted before transmission and are intended only to facilitate delivery to the intended recipient. Ghost404 is designed to automatically remove relayed encrypted messages after successful delivery or after they expire.

Ghost404 does not intentionally maintain permanent server-side archives of private conversations.

Although Ghost404 is designed to remove temporary relay data automatically, temporary delays may occur due to synchronization, network conditions, or circumstances beyond our reasonable control.

10. Voice and Video Calls

Ghost404 provides encrypted voice and video calling functionality. Whenever technically possible, media streams are transmitted directly between participants using peer-to-peer communication.

Ghost404 does not intentionally record, monitor, permanently store, or otherwise retain the contents of voice or video calls.

Call quality may vary depending on network conditions, Internet service providers, device capabilities, and other technical factors outside the Developer's control.

11. Encryption

Ghost404 is designed to protect communications using encryption during transmission. Where supported by the application's architecture, messages and calls are encrypted before being transmitted between users.

While we implement reasonable security practices intended to protect communications, no method of electronic communication or data storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure. Accordingly, Ghost404 cannot guarantee absolute security under all circumstances.

12. Analytics and Diagnostics

  • Feature usage statistics.

Analytics do not intentionally include:

  • Files exchanged between users.
