Easy QR Studio ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

We do not collect any personal data such as your name, contact details, or location. However, we collect anonymous data for analytics and performance improvement purposes. This includes:

While the app may request access to your device's camera due to included libraries, Easy QR Studio does not use or access your camera in any way. We are working to remove unnecessary permissions in future updates.

The app connects to the internet for analytics and performance monitoring. No personal information is transmitted or stored through this connection.

No login or account creation is required to use the app. We do not collect usernames, passwords, or other authentication data.

9. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent any user data to third parties. Data collected via Firebase or Google services is governed by their respective policies.
