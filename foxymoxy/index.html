Welcome to FoxyMoxy ("Game"). FoxyMoxy is a platformer game. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you play our game.

3. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you play the game:

  • Analytics Data: Anonymous analytics data (such as gameplay events and crash reports) used to improve the game experience.
  • Device Information: Basic technical information such as device model, operating system version, and language settings.
  • Advertising Identifiers: If advertising is enabled, an advertising ID may be collected to serve and measure ads (see Section 7).

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, email address, or other personal contact information to play the game.

4. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the game.
  • To analyze game performance and fix bugs.
  • To display advertisements, if enabled.
  • To process in-app purchases, if enabled.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in the following locations:

  • Locally: Game progress and settings are stored on your device.
  • Cloud Services: Anonymous analytics data is processed by Firebase.

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Permissions Required

The game requires the following permission:

  • Internet Access: Used for analytics, loading advertisements (if enabled), and processing in-app purchases (if enabled).

7. Advertising

FoxyMoxy may include third-party advertising (for example, Google AdMob). Ad providers may collect and use device identifiers, such as your advertising ID, to serve personalized or non-personalized ads and to measure their performance. You can reset or limit your advertising ID in your device settings.

8. In-App Purchases

FoxyMoxy may offer optional in-app purchases. Purchases are processed by the app store (for example, Google Play). We do not receive or store your payment card details; they are handled directly by the store's payment system.

9. Third-Party Services

The game may use the following third-party services, each with its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase – analytics and crash reporting.
  • Google AdMob – advertising, if enabled.
  • Google Play – in-app purchases, if enabled.

10. Children's Privacy

FoxyMoxy is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

11. Data Deletion

Because game progress is stored locally, you can remove all associated data by uninstalling the game from your device. To request deletion of any analytics data, contact us at the email below.

12. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as necessary. Any significant changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
